Problem Statement
Create a program that determines how many years you have left 
until retirement and the year you can retire. 
It should prompt for your current age and the age you want to retire 
and display the output based on the example given below.


====================
Sample Output
What is your current age? 25
At what age would you like to retire? 65
You have 40 years left until you can retire.
It's 2020, so you can retire in 2060.


====================
Constraints
Figure out how to get the current year in JavaScript
If the user enters incorrect data, such as string or character
If the user enters retiring age (65)
How to convert the input to numerical value before the calculations

====================
Inputs:
current age
retirement age


Processes:
calculate the retirement year
calculate years left

Outputs:
retirement year
years left

====================
Test Plan
Input:
   current age: 25
   retirement age: 65

Expected result:
   years left: 40
   retirement year: 2060
